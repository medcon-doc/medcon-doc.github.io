Manual Item Identification

This section gives a description on the thematic classification of all media content that was not available as plain text data.

In a first step student coders read through each newspaper edition, following the same selection protocol used in for the online coding and identified the universe of relevant articles in our manually collected newspapers.

For each relevant article the coders documented date, the name of the outlet, the starting page, and the title of the article in the ‘Tracking Sheets’. In a second step a random sample was drawn for each outlet. For validation purposes all selected articles were double checked by three independent coders and the final selection confirmed by majority vote of those three coders. In a last step the final selection of articles were digitalized and added to the final sample.
